About Us

David Gould Valeting was established in August 2008 as a mobile Valeting service servicing both private & public sectors. We developed a massive customer base in the early years & we still have that huge following today.We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service and extremely high standards.
In November 2013 we based ourselves @ Texaco/Mace Wilkinstown, Navan where we operate Co. Meath’s busiest Vehicle Wash & Valeting centers. We recently acquired a second location also located just outside Navan @ The Applegreen service station on the Navan ~ Slane Rd.
We're passionate about supporting our community. In total we employ 6 local people & support local schools, events & fundraisers.
