About Us

David Gould Valeting was established in August 2008 as a mobile Valeting service servicing both private & public sectors. We developed a massive customer base in the early years & we still have that huge following today.

In November 2013 we based ourselves @ Texaco/Mace Wilkinstown, Navan where we operate Co. Meath’s busiest Vehicle Wash & Valeting centers.
We open 7 days a week & bank holidays. Our opening hours are from 09:00 ~ 18:00 Monday to Saturday & Sunday’s & Bank Holidays from 10:00 ~ 17:00

We are a business with strong community values. In total we employ 6 local people & we love giving back to the community by supporting local schools and being involved with events & fundraisers.
In 2017 we held our 1st ever Daffodil Day fundraiser car wash & we raised €1090 for cancer research in Ireland, this year 2018 we smashed the previous amount by over €200, raising €1300.



When you put your hand in your pocket, take out your money and hand it to us you can expect very high standards from us. We’re always contactable through our Facebook page “David Gould Valeting” on the phone or through the contact form on this site.

We hope to see you soon!
